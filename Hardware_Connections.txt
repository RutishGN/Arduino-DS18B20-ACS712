DS18B20 Temperature Sensor:
Connect the DS18B20 sensor's VCC (Power) pin to 5V on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the DS18B20 sensor's GND (Ground) pin to GND on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the DS18B20 sensor's DATA (Signal) pin to digital pin 2 on the Arduino Uno.

ACS712 Current Sensor:
Connect the ACS712 module's VCC to 5V on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the ACS712 module's GND to GND on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the ACS712 module's OUT pin to analog pin A3 on the Arduino Uno.

I2C 16x2 LCD Display (If still connected, as per previous instructions):
Connect the I2C display's SDA pin to the A4 pin on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the I2C display's SCL pin to the A5 pin on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the I2C display's VCC to 5V on the Arduino Uno.
Connect the I2C display's GND to GND on the Arduino Uno.
